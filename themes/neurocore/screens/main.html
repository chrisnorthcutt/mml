Memory Bootcamp Playbook

The Memory Bootcamp Playbook is a comprehensive guide filled with education about your brain and resources designed to improve optimal brain function as you age. Education and coaching about nutrition, exercise, and sleep, among other things is provided at each session and is tracked in your playbook. As you progress through the program the playbook provides additional information about activities you can engage with that will continue to enhance your brain health and maximize your experience in the Memory Bootcamp Program.
